Class: ComplexVariation

A categorical variation domain jointly characterized by two or more other categorical variation domains.

URI: GA4GHCategoricalVariationDefinitions:ComplexVariation

Slots

Name Range Cardinality Description Info
catvars_id NONE 0..1 Categorical Variation Id. MUST be unique within document. .
catvars_type NONE 1..1 MUST be "ComplexVariation". .
operands CategoricalVariation 1..* None .
operator OperatorOptions 1..1 The logical operation applied to evaluating the object operands. MUST be "AND" or "OR". .
